Our Venues

Caramel Catering

Somerley House

Somerley is the jewel in Hampshire’s crown; a stunning privately owned Stately Home set in the heart of a 7,000 acre Estate on the Hampshire/Dorset border.

Available for exclusive hire – whether you dream of laid back opulence in the Main House up, a stunning marquee on the lawns or a boho inspired event in The Walled Garden or Woodlands, Somerley’s friendly and experienced Events Team can help you create your perfect day. Overnight accommodation also available within the main house or glamping!

Gordleton Mill

Set alongside the river, The Mill is the perfect place to relax with family and friends, enjoy delicious homemade food or unwind with a peaceful stay in one of our twelve stunning bedrooms. Located in the hamlet of Gordleton, just minutes from the New Forest and the South Coast, there’s plenty to discover right on our doorstep. Or simply stay at The Mill and explore our beautiful gardens and grounds, including our very own ‘Secret Garden’, full of art works, sculptures and exhibits.

“Paradise. Every detail of the hotel, restaurant bar has been well thought out…with your every detail attended to. Stunning grounds and gardens with river walks and hidden places to sit and let the world go by, great opportunity to see some amazing wildlife.

Pithall Barn

Set within 2,500 acres of beautiful Hampshire countryside on the border of Berkshire, Pitt Hall Barn is the perfect venue for weddings.

Pitt Hall Barn provides the setting for a wonderfully unique wedding. Licensed for civil ceremonies, Pitt Hall Barn and Spilman’s Copse woodland gives couples the opportunity to enjoy their whole day in one venue – from the intimate ceremony in the traditional barn or alfresco in the stunning woodland, to champagne and canapés in the garden, through into the dining barn to eat under the stars before dancing the night away to their chosen band or DJ.

HEATHFIELD HOUSE

Heathfield House is a stunning 12 bedroom manor house, sleeping 24 guests. Heathfield is set within a 15 acre private estate located in the beautiful National Park of The New Forest. Heathfield has been lovingly and extensively restored to provide understated elegance and comfort defining your family holiday, gathering or corporate retreat.

WARBORNE FARM

Warborne Farm is a 100 acre family-run farm. As well as being a great place to live and farm it is a wonderful location to stay in our lovingly converted boutique barns.

If you have always dreamt of a country wedding then Warborne farm is the right place for you. Our venue offers a rustic, rural and relaxed base for a unique boutique wedding. Having the venue privately for a whole weekend is a big part of a successful wedding. Being able to spend time with family and friends rather than just cramming it all into one day is proved to be a winner. All barns together sleep up to 40 people and combined with the option of camping in the orchard, it gives the opportunity for many guests to enjoy your special weekend to the fullest.

BARFORD PARK

At Barford Park, you’re guaranteed – A personal approach: We are a family run business and your wedding is of great importance to us. Exclusive use of the grounds and gardens for your celebration. There will be no other weddings or events on the same day. Stunning views of the Avon Valley. A beautiful setting with lots of great photo opportunities also. A unique wedding day in a 17th century listed barn: Make your wedding one to remember! Venue only: We provide the venue and you organise all other elements yourself.

SOPLEY LAKES

Set in a beautiful idyllic woodland location on the edge of The New Forest, Sopley Lake is the ideal venue for your wedding reception, special party or corporate event and is available to hire from mid April to October.

We have a large, secluded area to the side of the Lake which is surrounded by trees where a tent or marquee can be placed, with stunning views of the lake. We also have to offer ample parking, toilets and fresh water. Our shepherds hut or log cabin accommodation can also be hired for your party if you wish to stay over.

WEDDING IN THE WOODS

A fabulous venue set within the woodland wonderland of the New Forest National Park, with an expanse of green lawns and woodland areas. To accommodate the unpredictable weather there is a large indoor space that can fit up to 150 guests during the day and further guests during the evening. We impose very few rules and encourage plenty of original ideas. The way that you choose to use the facilities that we provide is designed to be flexible. After all, it is your special day! We want you to feel that your woodland wedding really is your dream come true!

NEW HOUSE ESTATE

A stunning Grade I listed, Jacobean Manor House found fifteen minutes South of the historic city of Salisbury on the Wiltshire/Hampshire border. We are approached by a long avenue lined with lime and cherry trees, and boast wonderful lawns and stunning surrounding woodland. Built in 1609, Newhouse was designed in a Y shape to represent the three arms of the Trinity and is one of only two ‘trinity’ houses in Britain. The Georgian wings were added in the 18th Century and these two large rooms have played a major part in the success of our hosting events over the last 25 years. Since 1633, the house and estate have remained in the same family and is now home to the Jeffreys family.

SOPLEY MIL

A beautiful, newly renovated watermill, surrounded on both sides by the Avon River, with uninterrupted views across the water meadows. Exclusively yours, Sopley Mill is a blank canvas providing privacy, and flexibility for you to create your own unique wedding. The Mill is licensed for weddings for up to 100 people, and can accommodate another 40 evening guests. There is no corkage charge on wines and we have a pay bar available. We have the gorgeous “Avon Suite” on site for a bride or groom to use to get ready in, and for the duration of your wedding.
