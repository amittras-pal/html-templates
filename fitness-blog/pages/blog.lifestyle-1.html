The 5 Best Memorial Day Mattress Sales to Shop This Weekend

Kathleen Ferraro March 29, 2022

Your mattress is the literal foundation of a good night's sleep. And if your current bed isn't cutting it, now is the perfect time to shop for a new one during this weekend's Memorial Day mattress sales.

1. Nest Bedding Owl Natural Latex Hybrid Mattress

You don't have to choose between plushness and support with this Nest Bedding mattress. The quilted foam top provides comfort, while the latex foam foundation cradles your curves.

And that's not even the best part — right now, you can buy the bed for 25 percent off during Nest Bedding's Memorial Day Sale through June 6. During the sale, the mattress also comes with a free Lifetime Comfort Bundle that includes two pillows and one free foam topper exchange if you don't like the feel of your original mattress.

You can pair your new mattress with comfy bedding, including the Silk Cloud Comforter that's also currently on sale for 35 percent off.

2. Serta Arctic Mattress

Save as much as $1,000 on a Serta mattress and base (that's up to $400 off the mattress and up to $600 off the base) during the brand's Memorial Day sale, running through June 14.

If you're a hot sleeper, their temperature-moderating Arctic Mattress can help keep you cool (and asleep) all night long.

You can also browse other beds, including Serta's popular bed-in-a-box, their iComfort hybrid mattress and more.

3. Beautyrest Black Mattress

If you prefer a more supported snooze, Beautyrest has got your back (literally). The Beautyrest Black line provides premier support to help keep you comfortable all night long. The mattress also includes memory foam to ease any remaining pressure so you get the most quality rest possible.

Save up to $1,000 on a Beautyrest Black mattress and base combo (that's up to $600 off the mattress and $400 off the base) during the brand's Memorial Day sale, which continues through June 14.

4. Tuft & Needle Hybrid Mattress

Balance softness and support with Tuft & Needle's Hybrid Mattress, one of the bedding company's most popular picks (as told by nearly 1,000 happy reviewers).

You can score up to $500 off the bed (and up to $450 off other Tuft & Needle mattresses) during the Memorial Day promo through May 30.

Dress up your new mattress with a topper, pillows, duvet and more, all of which are also on sale for up to 15 percent off through Memorial Day.

5. Purple Hybrid Premier 3 Mattress

Convenience is a given if you're buying a mattress from Purple, a popular bed-in-a-box company that delivers your purchase right to your doorstep.

And now you can save $300 when you buy the brand's bestselling Hybrid Premier 3 Mattress on sale through June 7. As a bonus, you can also save $500 on the Ascent Adjustable Base when you buy any mattress — consider your bedroom complete.
