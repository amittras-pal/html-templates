Relaxing Arunachal with Nameri National Park Tour

Package Description

Be prepared to wander in the realms of abundant nature, unwinding setting and the mighty Himalayas found on the land of Arunachal Pradesh and also to adore the wilderness of Nameri National Park. With Buddhist monasteries, exquisite natural beauty and a relaxed pace of life, Arunachal Pradesh is one of the most beautiful travel destinations in North East India. And with our Arunachal Pradesh holiday packages, we assure to give you a kind of holiday time that one should have while in this beautiful state. This 8 nights 9 days Arunachal Tour with Nameri National Park is overbrimming with a range of experience, whether nature, pilgrimage, culture, sightseeing, wildlife or adventure. Starting from Guwahati (Assam) and Bhalukpong, our holiday package further takes you to the various alluring attraction of Dirang, Tawang, and Bomdila. Not just this, this expedition also includes a visit to some lesser-known yet, alluring passes - Bum La and Sela. Talking about Nameri Eco Park, it is a getaway for all the bird watchers and nature lovers seeking an ultimate vacation time. On the foothills of Eastern Himalayas, Nameri is home to more than 370 species of birds. But thatâ€™s not it, what makes it a must-visit place is the unique experience of bird watching while rafting through the Jia Bhoroli River. This 8N/9N Arunachal Pradesh with Nameri Eco Park itinerary gives you the chance to relish that experience. For a brief insight of our tour package, check out the below explained day-wise itinerary.

9 Days

Itinerary

Day 1

ARRIVAL IN GUWAHATI

Your trip with Tour My India begins with your arrival at Guwahati Airport/Station.At the airport, our representative will be waiting to welcome you. He will transfer you to a pre-booked hotel in Guwahati.After checking in to the hotel, get freshen up, and overcome the jet lag.In the afternoon, be ready to go on a pilgrimage tour in Guwahati where you will visit Kamakhya Temple, Balaji Temple, and Umananda Temple.Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2

GUWAHATI TO BHALUKPONG

In the morning, you will be served with a delicious breakfast.Followed by breakfast, commence a 241 km drive to Bhalukpong - a beautiful small village in Arunachal Pradesh.Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a pre-booked hotel.Dinner and overnight stay will be provided at the hotel.

Day 3

BHALUKPONG TO DIRANG

Enjoy a nutritious breakfast in the morning.After that, begin a 139 km drive to Dirang which would take around 4-5 hours to complete.On the way, stop at Orchid Research Centre at Tipi for an informative tour. The research centre is known to exhibit an extensive range of orchid species that are found in India. From there, continue your drive to Dirang.To reach Tawang via Bhalukpong, seek ILP permission at Bhalukpong Gate.In the afternoon, go on a village tour to Sherdukpen. This village is known for its people who practice Mahayana sect of Buddhism.Followed by a village tour, continue your drive to Dirang, En-route, visit Upper Monastery in Bomdila.After reaching Dirang, you will be accommodated in a pre-booked hotel.Dinner and overnight stay will be provided at the hotel.

Day 4

DIRANG TO TAWANG

Day 4 of this fantastic tour will start an early morning drive to Tawang. From Dirang, Tawang is located at a distance of 128 km, and hence, it would be a 4-5 hours drive.En route, visit the famous Hot Water Spring in Dirang. Followed by this, enjoy a delectable breakfast.Lunch will also be provided on the way. Post lunch, resume the drive to Tawang. On the way, stop at Sela Pass. From Sela Pass, you can capture the charming views of the great Himalayas.After that, visit the renowned Jaswantghar Museum - a war memorial dedicated to Jaswant Singh Rathore, an Indian Rifleman of 4 Garhwal Rifles.On arrival at Tawang, check-in to a pre-booked hotel. Dinner and overnight stay will be provided at the hotel.

Day 5

TAWANG TO BUMLA

Post breakfast, commence an expedition to Bum La which is located at a distance of 25 km from Tawang.Bum La Pass has an immense historical significance. It was Bum La where one the vicious battles of 1962 Sino-Indian War took place.Visit the famous Sangestar Tso Lake, popularly known as Madhuri Lake. The lake is named after the veteran actress - Madhuri Dixit who has shot one of her movies, Koyla, at this place.After exploring Bum La Pass and Madhuri Lake, return to the hotel.Dinner and overnight stay will be provided at the hotel.

Day 6

SIGHTSEEING IN TAWANG

Enjoy a delicious breakfast in the morning.Followed by this, go on a sightseeing tour of Tawang where you will visit the famous attractions of the region - Gumpha Monastery, War Memorial and Buddhist Statue.Once done exploring the town, head back to the hotel.Later, in the evening, you will be taken for Light and Sound Show. On the way, explore the local market of the town.For dinner and overnight stay, return to the hotel.

Day 7

TAWANG TO BOMDILA

In the morning, you will be served with breakfast.Post breakfast, begin a drive to Bomdila - the headquarters of the West Kameng in Arunachal Pradesh. The place is known for its picturesque setting which boasts scenic views of lofty ranges of eastern Himalayas.On arrival at Bomdila, check-in to a pre-booked hotel.Later, in the evening, be ready to visit the famous Lower Gompa and Emporium Local Market.For dinner and overnight stay, head back to the hotel.

Day 8

BOMDILA TO NAMERI

Like every day, enjoy a delectable breakfast in the morning.Followed by this, begin a 122 km drive to Nameri National Park.Upon arrival, check-in to a pre-booked hotel.Dinner and overnight stay will be provided at the hotel.

Day 9

DEPARTURE

Post breakfast, enjoy river rafting on Jia Bhoroli.Jia Bhoroli river is a part of Nameri National Park. The river also provided an excellent opportunity for angling activity for the golden Mahseer.In the afternoon, begin a drive to the onward destination (Guwahati Airport/Station).Upon arrival, proceed to board your flight/train back home.
