The Mesmerizing Traditions of Nagaland

Package Description

None can disagree the fact that Nagaland is truly a land of wonders. Its peaceful environment, colourful festivals and varieties of tribal people entices every explorer to visit Nagaland. This extremely rich in culture destination is the reason why we have prepared a 10 nights 11 days Nagaland cultural tour package. In this tour you can explore the historical village of Khonoma in Kohima and observe the lifestyle of Ao Naga, Lotha, Longma and Lungso tribe. Also, you get an opportunity to interact with the tribes of Longwa, Konyak and Tuensang. We will guide you to places such as Dimapur, Kohima, Mokokchung, Tuensang, Mon and Dibrugarh to witness many faces of lifestyle, food, people of this enchanting land. Our package will give you an opportunity to savour local delicacies and you will also be taken to sites in Assam such as Koli Aai Thaan, Dehing Satra and the lush Tea Gardens in Sivasagar.

11 Days

Itinerary

Day 1

DELHI - DIMAPUR

On arrival at Dimapur airport, our representative will be there for your welcome and after then you will be transferred to hotel. After resting for some time you can go for sightseeing. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2

DIMAPUR SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast, you will be taken to explore the ruins of the Ahoms dynasty which dates back to the 13th century. After lunch, you will be driven 13 km to the Diezephe Craft Village. Here, you can witness craftsmen indulged in wood carving and weaving. Head back to the hotel in Dimapur for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3

On day 3, after you check out from the hotel you will be driven to Kohima. On arrival, check in at the hotel and after freshening and having lunch, you can venture out and sightsee Kohima War Cemetery. It was build in memory of the soldiers who lost their lives during the World War II. Return to the hotel, dinner and overnight stay will follow.

Day 4

KOHIMA

You will be taken to Khonoma village which is a historical village and also the last base for the Angami tribe. Later, you will be taken to visit the state museum where you can witness the tribal lifestyle and culture of the Naga people. This will be followed by dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Kohima.

Day 5

KOHIMA - MOKOKCHUNG

You will be driven to Mokokchung, a beautiful destination of the Ao tribe and en route you can visit Wokha, the Lotha tribal village. On arrival in Mokokchung you can check in at the selected hotel and after freshening up you can go explore the town. Overnight stay at the hotel in Mokokchung.

Day 6

MOKOKCHUNG - TUENSANG

After breakfast you will be driven to Tuensang en route you will be visiting Longma and Lungso villages inhabited by Ao Naga tribe. As you reach, you can stay at the selected hotel in Tuensang.

Day 7

TUENSANG

You can go explore the villages that are inhabited by different tribes. The major tribes around Tuensang are Chang, Khiamniungan, Sangtam, Yimchunger and Phom. You can interact with the locals and learn about their lifestyle. This will be followed by dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 8

TUENSANG - MON

As you are over with your breakfast in Tuensang, you will be driving to Mon. On the way, you will visit the villages that are inhabited by Konyak tribes. This tribe is famously known as headhunters. On arrival at Mon town, you can check in at the selected hotel.

Day 9

MON

The entire day, you can roam around Mon. You will be taken to explore the Longwa village which is one of the biggest villages of the district and it is also located in the Indo-Myanmar border (also visit the legendary twin tree). Later, return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 10

MON - DIBRUGARH

After breakfast, you will be driven to Dibrugarh, Assam and as you reach, check in at the selected hotel and spend the day free for leisure activities. You can also go sightsee some attractions like the tea gardens, koli aai thaan, dehing satra etc. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 11

DIBRUGARH - DELHI

You will be driven to the airport after breakfast to connect with your onward journey to Delhi.
