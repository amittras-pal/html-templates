Relaxing Golfing with GetSetGo

Package Description

It is about time you let our team create the legendary experience that you never imagined was possible. Set amidst lush landscapes and natural surrounds, Kaziranga Golf Resort is an 18 hole championship course that is conceived as a premium heritage resort for spending a holiday in a colonial tea environment. The challenging 18-hole, par 71 golf course in the midst of a tea garden is a first of its kind in the world and developed on a 150 acre site.

The Golf Resort is a destination in and of itself with breathtaking, panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, heritage tea estates and bungalows, and a menu featuring high quality, locally sourced ingredients. Extend your golf trip to see the wildlife up and close in an elephant or jeep safari at Kaziranga National Park and spend a refined evening by the mighty Brahmaputra. The view will be a perfect side to your meal.

Whether you are looking for a weekend golf vacation or a fun day of play with an unmatched wildlife and dining experience we have something for you!
