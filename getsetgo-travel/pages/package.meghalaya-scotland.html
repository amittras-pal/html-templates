Explore the Scotland of East Shillong

Package Description

If you are a nature lover and an adventure seeker, then this trip to Shillong is perfect for you. While you are here, you will enjoy a spectacular sight of Umiam Lake from your luxurious resort. Here, you are free to enjoy the bustling night-life of the city, colorful markets, and unabashed beauty of nature lying just below the city line.

Shillong offers you the freedom of walking down to the random waterfalls or trekking a day at David Scott Trail. While visiting Cherrapunji, the wettest place on the earth, you get to walk over the living root bridges and see million-years old limestone cave. The majestic wildlife of Kaziranga National Park is just the cherry on the top.

8 Days

Itinerary

Day 1

Guwahati

Touch down to Guwahati airport and meet our tour guide driver. Transfer to hotel to freshen up. Enjoy Sun Set River Cruise on the Brahmaputra. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 2

Guwahati- Shillong

After breakfast proceed to Shillong by car (Distance 100 km; Travel Time: About 3 hrs). On the way, travel to Umiam Lake, Elephant Falls, Shillong Peak, Don Bosco Museum. Overnight stay in Shillong.

Day 3

Shillong- Dawki

Breakfast at the hotel. After that proceed to Dawki Bangladesh Border (Distance: 90 km; Travel Time: About 3 hr). Enjoy boating and site seeing. Travel to Mawlynnong, the cleanest village of Asia. Overnight stay in Shillong.

Day 4

Shillong-Cherrapunji

Proceed to Cherrapunji (Distance 65 km; Travel Time: Around 2 hrs) after breakfast. En-route visit Seven Sisters Falls, Maswami Cave, Nonakalkai Water Falls, Eco Park. Overnight stay in Shillong.

Day 5

Shillong- Kaziranga National Park

After breakfast start for Kaziranga National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site (Distance 260 km; Travel Time: Around 4 hr). Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 6

Kaziranga National Park

Start your day early (5:30 am) to enjoy elephant safari. Get back to the hotel for breakfast. After breakfast, free time for your activities. After lunch (1:30 pm) enjoy the jeep safari. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 7

Kaziranga National Park- Guwahati

Proceed to Guwahati (Distance 220 km; Travel Time: about 4 hrs) after having breakfast. En-route visit Guwahati Town, local markets, Shankar Dev Kalakshetra. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 8

Guwahati Tour and Airport Transfer

After breakfast, check out from the hotel to visit Maa Kamakhya Temple, Balaji Temple, Brahmaputra River. After Lunch transfer to Guwahati Airport for your flight.
