Romance in Breathtaking Arunachal Pradesh

Package Description

With its sprawling emerald meadows and sparkling rivers, Arunachal Pradesh is the perfect getaway for couples of every age. The trip includes a trip to Dirang Dzong Valley, Dirang Monastery, Monap Tribal Village, Sela Pass, Bumla Pass China border and Tawang Monastery.

The long rides in the winding roads, the hot cuppa noodles in the roadside shack, and the croonings played in your car stereo will create forever lasting memories for you. Any trip to the northeastern states is incomplete without a wild safari in the much loved Kaziranga National Park.

In this trip, you will have a chance to meet the one-horned giants. If you are adventurous enough, you will get a chance to try your hands at river rafting in the wild rapids of Nameri National Park. To absorb the beauty of the wild nature, you get to stay in an Eco Village in the National Park.

10 Days

Itinerary

Day 1

Guwahati- Kaziranga National Park

Our expert tour guide and driver will meet you at Guwahati Airport. After the meet, proceed to Kaziranga National Park (distance 220 km- 4 hrs). En-route visit to Nagaon, have lunch and take some rest. Carry on the journey to Kaziranga, check-in hotel./ Overnight stay at Kaziranga.

Day 2

Kaziranga National Park

Start Your day with an elephant safari early in the morning (5:30 am) at Khora range. Spot wildlife and enjoy the untouched beauty of nature. After breakfast, you are free to explore on your own. In the afternoon (1;30 pm) exciting jeep safari deep into the forest. Overnight stay at Kaziranga.

Day 3

Kaziranga National Park- Bhalukpong

Proceed to Bhalukpong (distance: 130 km; time: 3hrs) after breakfast. En-route travel to Tippi Orchids; the third largest research center of Asia. Hotel check-in and overnight stay in Bhalukpong.

Day 4

Bhalukpong-Dirang

Breakfast at hotel then proceeds to Dirang (distance 130km; time 3hrs). Visit Dirang Dzong Valley, Dirang Monastery, Monap Tribal Villages. Enjoy local cuisine at Dirang Town. Overnight stay at Dirang.

Day 5

Dirang-Tawang

After breakfast go to Tawang (distance 75 km; time 2 hrs), the mountain town of Asia. Visit Jung waterfalls, Jaswant Singh War Memorial, Sela Pass on the way. Check-in hotel and take rest. Overnight stay at Tawang.

Day 6

Tawang

Have breakfast and then travel to Bumla Pass to see the indo-china Border. Visit Madhuri Lake (named after Indian Actress Madhuri Dixit) and enjoy breathtaking nature view. Come back to the city and travel to Tawang Monastery; the second largest monastery of the world. In the afternoon, visit the Indo-China war memorial, Taktsang Gompa. Visit the local town and check the local market. Overnight stay at Tawang.

Day 7

Tawang-Bomdila

Start the journey to Bomdila (distance 200 km; time 4 hrs) after having breakfast. Visit stunning Bomdila mountain viewpoint, Bomdila Monastery, and check the local markets and towns. Overnight stay at Bomdila.

Day 8

Bomdila-Tezpur

Start your journey towards Tezpur (distance 200 km; time 4 hrs) after having breakfast at the hotel. On the way visit ancient hillocks, Agnigarh Hills, Kolia Bhomara Bridge. Have lunch on the way. Visit the local markets. Overnight stay at Tezpur.

Day 9

Guwahati Airport/Railway Transfer

After breakfast transfer to Guwahati Airport or railway station.

Day 10

Dimapur – Guwahati

After breakfast start for Guwahati airport for your flight.
