Attendance/Missed Classes Policy

While we take many precautions, time to time we may cancel classes. It may happen due to many reasons such as an emergency for the teacher, technological issues preventing logins, Power outages etc.

At the same time, a student may also miss a class for any reason.

If we cancel a class, we will either give a credit on the subscription or move the subscription renewal date at the prorated hourly rate for that class.

If the student misses a class, as a courtesy we make the recording of that class available. Since it is a manual process to record the class, there is a chance that the teacher may miss to hit the record button. In this scenario, we will offer a 30 mins catchup class on request only.

Catchup classes are to be scheduled for Friday evenings only and within 3 weeks of incident occurrence. We reserve the right to invite more students to that class if we choose to do so.
